How Guardia protects you

Scammers plant fake transactions in your history hoping you'll copy the wrong address. Guardia stops that.

1

The attack

A scammer sends a $0 transaction from an address that looks almost identical to yours. It shows up in your history — bait for your next copy-paste.

0xd8dA...96045 // scammer (lookalike)
0xd8dA...96045 // you (real)
2

Instant detection

Guardia has learned to recognize these poisoning patterns. The moment a page loads, every suspicious transaction is identified and blocked — no configuration needed.

// page loaded:
🛡️ Shield active — 31 threats detected
3

Your choice of protection

Pick how threats are handled. Guard mode hides poison transactions completely — they vanish from the page. Scan mode highlights them in red so you can inspect them yourself.

// toggle modes:
🔒 Guard — threats hidden
🔎 Scan — threats highlighted
